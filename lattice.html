Test: do quasars form a lattice with Earth at a site?

If quasars sat on a cubic lattice and Earth occupied one of its nodes, every "shell" would consist of points at fixed integer distances r² = a²+b²+c². Such shells contain a highly regular polyhedral pattern of directions (4, 6, 8, 12, 24 points at fixed angles), not a scatter.
