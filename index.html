Varshni 1976 — Quasar Redshift Shells

Each translucent sphere is one of the 57 redshift groups from Table I of Varshni's paper. Under the cosmological interpretation of redshift, members of a group sit at the same distance from Earth and therefore on a spherical shell centered on Earth.

Earth is the small blue marker at the origin. Shell radius is proportional to redshift z. Colored points are individual quasars; same color = same group.

Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · right-drag to pan · hover a quasar for its name.

Source: Y. P. Varshni, Astrophys. Space Sci. 43, 3–8 (1976).
