Test: are group members clustered on the sky?

If the redshift coincidences came from local clustering, members of the same group would lie near each other in the sky. The plot below puts every quasar at its catalog RA/Dec; lines connect members of the same group.

Solid dots = position parsed from name (e.g. PKS 0119-04). Hollow dots = catalog-only name (3C 191, PHL 938); their angular position is unknown to this dataset and shown at a hashed location for completeness.

Varshni 1976, possibility (1): "An examination of the coordinates ... shows that in most cases there is no such correlation." Only the solid-dot subset can be inspected here.
